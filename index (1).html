Chocolate Cake !

Prep Time: 50 min

Servings: 5 Plates

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and cocoa.
  3. Add eggs and milk, stir well.
  4. Pour into pan and bake for 30 mins.
  5. Let it cool and serve.
